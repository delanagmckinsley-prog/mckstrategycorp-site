About Mckinsley Strategy & Consulting Co.

Strategic advisory services designed to help leaders navigate growth, complexity, transformation, and workforce evolution with confidence.

Mckinsley Strategy & Consulting Co. is a boutique advisory firm focused on workforce strategy, organizational effectiveness, leadership enablement, HR transformation, and enterprise change.

We work with organizations that need thoughtful, practical, executive-level support to solve critical people and business challenges — from operating model redesign and talent strategy to culture transformation and large-scale organizational change.

Our approach blends strategic clarity with real-world execution, helping clients build strong foundations for growth, resilience, and long-term value creation.

Founder

Delana McKinsley, MBA

Delana is a senior People & Culture executive and transformation leader with experience advising and leading across insurance, energy, utilities, technology, and professional services.

She brings deep expertise in workforce strategy, organization design, leadership development, HR operating model modernization, and enterprise change — with a strong track record of helping organizations translate strategy into measurable business outcomes.
